Skater Showdown

This game is a spin off of the classic skateboarding game "Skate". The rules are as follows: The players try to complete tricks without making mistakes. The first to complete a trick successfully has "set" the trick. The opponent now has to complete the same trick without making a mistake otherwise he gets a letter (example:"S"). The round starts over, and whoever gathers all the letters "SKATE" first LOSES!

1. Choose character.
2. Press and hold the trick letters for 1 second to execute trick. Good Luck!
